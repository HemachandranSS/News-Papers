### **Overview of ICICI Bank**
ICICI Bank is India's second-largest private-sector bank. It offers a wide range of financial products and services to retail (individuals), SME (small and medium enterprises), and corporate customers. Through its subsidiaries, the bank also operates in life insurance, general insurance, housing finance, and other financial services.

---

### **Key Highlights for 9MFY24 (April to December 2023)**  

#### **Financial Performance**
1. **Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR):** 16.03%  
   - This shows the bank's financial strength and ability to absorb losses.
   
2. **Net Interest Margin (NIM):** 4.57%  
   - NIM is the difference between interest earned and interest paid, indicating profitability.

3. **Non-Performing Assets (NPA):**  
   - Gross NPA: 2.30% (before provisioning for bad loans)  
   - Net NPA: 0.44% (after provisioning)

4. **CASA Ratio:** 39.4%  
   - Indicates the proportion of low-cost current and savings accounts in total deposits.
