## **Simplification of Income Tax Law: What Does the New Bill Say?**  

India's existing **Income Tax Act, 1961**, has undergone multiple amendments, making it complex. To simplify the tax law, the **New Income Tax Bill, 2025**, was introduced in the Lok Sabha on **February 13, 2025**.  

### **Key Highlights of the New Income Tax Bill (2025):**  

1. **Reduction in Word Count and Page Length:**  
   - The **1961 Act** had **5,12,000 words**; the new bill reduces this to **2,60,000 words**.  
   - The total pages are reduced from **823** to **622**.  

2. **Changes in Sections & Chapters:**  
   - Old Act: **298 sections**  
   - New Bill: **536 sections** (increase)  
   - Old Act: **47 chapters**  
   - New Bill: **23 chapters** (simplification)  

3. **Changes in Schedules & Explanations:**  
   - Old Act: **18 schedules**  
   - New Bill: **57 schedules**  
   - The old law had **900 explanations**, which have now been removed for clarity.  

4. **Simplified Tax Calculation:**  
   - A **formula for tax calculation** is provided, allowing taxpayers to estimate their tax liability easily.  
   - The new law uses **simpler language** and concise sentences.  

5. **Terminology Changes:**  
   - The terms **Financial Year, Assessment Year, and Previous Year** are replaced by a single term: **Tax Year**.  
   - The **Tax Year** will be from **April 1 to March 31** to reduce confusion.  

6. **Virtual Digital Assets (VDA) Taxation:**  
   - Cryptocurrencies, **NFTs**, and other digital assets, which were taxed from **2022**, now have a **clear legal definition** in the new bill.  

7. **Income Tax Heads Remain Unchanged:**  
   - The five tax heads remain the same:  
     - Salary Income  
     - Income from House Property  
     - Profits & Gains from Business/Profession  
     - Capital Gains  
     - Other Sources Income  

8. **Revised Rules for Non-Profit Organizations (NPOs):**  
   - More **detailed tax structures** for non-profits.  
   - Compliance rules and **restrictions on commercial activities** are clarified.  

9. **Tax Benefits for Startups & Green Energy Investments:**  
   - **Startups, digital businesses, and renewable energy** investments will receive **capital gains tax benefits**.  

10. **Implementation Timeline:**  
   - The **Lok Sabha committee** will review and approve the bill.  
   - Once passed in **Parliament**, the new law will be **effective from April 1, 2026**.  

### **Conclusion:**  
The **New Income Tax Bill, 2025**, aims to simplify the existing law, reduce complexity, and make tax calculations easier. With **fewer explanations, fewer chapters, and clearer definitions**, taxpayers are expected to benefit from a **more structured and predictable tax regime**.
